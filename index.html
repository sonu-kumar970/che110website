Priority interventions — critical states
Punjab, Haryana, Rajasthan — emergency groundwater regulation
These states show >4 m/yr depletion driven by paddy and wheat irrigation. Immediate crop-diversification incentives, night-time irrigation bans, and canal lining are needed. CGWB recommends mandatory metering for all agricultural borewells above 5 HP.
Delhi & Chandigarh — urban aquifer protection
Urban over-extraction for domestic and industrial use has dropped the water table by 3+ m/yr. Mandatory rainwater harvesting for buildings >300 m², rooftop recharge pits, and treated wastewater recycling are recommended at city scale.
Gujarat, Maharashtra, UP — moderate-stress management
Micro-irrigation (drip/sprinkler) adoption to reduce farm water use by 30–40%. Promote watershed development and check-dam construction in semi-arid zones. Community-managed aquifer recharge programs aligned with Jal Shakti Abhiyan.
